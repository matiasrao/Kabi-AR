About

Lucas Khabie alias "Kabi (AR)" is a DJ and producer born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. He began his studies at the age of 10, playing the guitar, and later continued with percussion instruments, focusing on the improvisation and composition system with signs created by La Bomba de Tiempo, and body percussion under the direction of Santiago Ablin (El urban crash).

At the same time, he entered the world of electronic music listening to DJs like Hernán Cattaneo, Guy J, Nick Warren and many others. Under this influence, he began to perform as a DJ and a few months later as a producer, focusing on the Progressive House sound and its variants.

To this day, at 21, he has original productions released on labels such as Soundteller Records, and releases scheduled for the next few months on labels such as Clubsonica Records, Freegrant Music, Balkan Connection and Droid9.

His tracks have the support of international DJs of the genre such as Nick Varon and Hernán Cattaneo, who played one of his productions on episode 458 of Resident.

As a DJ, his sets seek to express a story that often involves various genres from Deep House, Progressive to Techno. Always focused on interesting and deep melodic lines combined with moving grooves.
